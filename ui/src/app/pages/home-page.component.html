AI Content Safety Platform

This platform helps teams evaluate text and image content with Azure AI Content Safety, route high-risk items for review, and tune moderation behavior for real-world business policies.

Watch platform walkthrough (primary) Microsoft Learn: Azure AI Content Safety

Custom Filters and Category Thresholds

You can configure per-category thresholds to decide whether content is safe, should be reviewed, or must be blocked. Each category uses severity levels 0, 2, 4, and 6, and thresholds can be tuned independently.

Violence

Detects threats, graphic harm, weapon usage, or instructions for violent acts. Use stricter thresholds to reduce exposure to violent material.

Self Harm

Flags self-injury, suicide encouragement, and harmful eating-disorder content. Set low thresholds for crisis-sensitive experiences.

Sexual Content

Identifies sexual language and explicit content, including high-risk sexual material. Tune thresholds by audience age and compliance needs.

Hate

Captures hateful or discriminatory expressions targeting protected groups. Lower thresholds help enforce stricter anti-abuse community standards.
